Agile Assessment and Coaching Plan - Executive Summary

Introduction

This assessment summarizes common patterns observed across multiple teams as part of an agile maturity review. The intent is not to evaluate or compare individual teams, but to surface organizational-level practices that require sustained leadership attention to improve predictability, value delivery, and alignment.

The findings are presented in an anonymized, parameter-based format to encourage constructive dialogue and focus on systemic behaviors rather than team-specific performance. These insights form the basis for a structured Stabilize, Adopt, and Optimized Agile Coaching Plan, designed to address near-term delivery risks while progressively strengthening execution discipline, governance clarity, and outcome-driven ways of working.

Assessment Insights

A detailed Agile Assessment was conducted as part of the Agile Transformation initiative to evaluate process maturity, delivery predictability, and flow efficiency. The assessment incorporated inputs from Product and Delivery organizations, along with self-assessments from Agile PODs.
One-on-one discussions with Product and Delivery stakeholders were held to capture qualitative insights and individual perspectives.

Each report presents a consolidated view across Product, Delivery, and POD teams against common assessment parameters, along with targeted recommendations from the Agile Coach.

Agile Coaching Approach Overview

  • The Agile Coaching approach will encompass coaching, training, and hands-on working sessions covering preparation and planning through execution supported by governance guardrails to ensure consistency and control.

  • The three-phase plan outlined below addresses both practices to sustain and strengthen, as well as areas requiring focused improvement. Each phase is designed to deliver defined outcomes, enabling leadership to track progress, quantify benefits, and build trust through sustained visibility and measurable results.

  • Detailed result sets derived from each Agile POD have been considered in shaping the three-phase approach.

  • Progress will be assessed and shared at regular intervals, with the plan reviewed and adjusted as needed to support continuous improvement.

    Assessment and Phase Outcome Summary

    The assessment reflects a maturing Agile foundation, with PI and sprint objectives in place and active stakeholder engagement supporting delivery.
    To fully realize business value, the focus should shift toward strengthening Outcome-driven execution, improving Backlog readiness and Prioritization, enforcing Scope stability, and driving Metrics-led decision making. Gen-AI is positioned as a key accelerator to enhance backlog management, enable quick wins, and improve planning effectiveness.

    Building on this baseline, the Stabilize-Adopt-Optimize & Scale Agile coaching journey enables a structured progression from stabilization to predictability and sustainable execution excellence.

    Potential Outcomes Across Phases:

  • Stabilize & Enable: Establish a clear delivery baseline with visibility into risks, dependencies, priorities, and core Agile disciplines.

  • Adopt & Embed: Embed execution discipline to improve planning rigor, sprint reliability, and delivery predictability through metrics-driven governance and Gen-AI enablement.

  • Optimize & Scale: Achieve sustained, scalable Agile execution with stable delivery, empowered teams, end-to-end visibility, and continuous improvement.

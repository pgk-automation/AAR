Agile Assessment Report: Planamation
Introduction

This Agile Assessment was conducted as part of the Agile Transformation initiative to evaluate Agile process maturity, execution predictability, and flow efficiency, while considering inputs from Product & Delivery organization.

This report captures opinions, challenges, and insights gathered through self-assessments from the POD team and feedback from key Product and Delivery stakeholders.

Its objective is to surface key observations from Product, Delivery, and Team maturity, along with recommendations from the Agile Coach to address gaps through targeted Agile Transformation.

The report also provides summarized takeaways across key dimensions for a quick executive snapshot.

Based on the consolidated assessments, a pragmatic Stabilize, Adopt, and Optimize-to-Scale improvement roadmap has been defined, with a clear focus on improving delivery predictability, quality, transparency, and measurable business value realization.

                  Summary
                  Enablement & Continuous Improvement
                  • Prioritize practical GenAI adoption by embedding it into operational workflows, Scrum events, story writing, acceptance criteria, and documentation to drive measurable productivity gains.
                  • Standardize mid-sprint change and sync protocols to improve adaptability, enforce impact assessment, and prevent unplanned scope absorption.
                  • Institutionalize continuous improvement by mandating actionable, time-bound retrospective outcomes supported by quantitative metrics and customer satisfaction insights.
                  • Strengthen learning and prevention mechanisms through actionable RCAs, shared learnings across teams, and targeted pilots of GenAI-assisted RCA for faster, higher-quality outcomes.
                  Engineering Excellence
                  • Demonstrate and scale effective test automation practices that measurably reduce manual testing effort and improve CI reliability.
                  • Modernize CI/CD pipelines to enhance speed, stability, and maintainability, while allocating explicit capacity for automation and platform improvements.
                  • Enforce static code analysis as a mandatory quality gate on every build to prevent defect leakage.
                  • Regularly inspect automation and pipeline maturity and take corrective action where inconsistencies persist.
                  Backlog & Planning Governance
                  • Establish strong backlog discipline through outcome-driven prioritization, enforced Definition of Ready/Done standards, and consistent backlog refinement aligned to PI goals.
                  • Complete shaping, dependency analysis, and prioritization before PI Planning to avoid spillover, unclear commitments, and reactive decision-making.
                  • Improve governance through clear demo objectives, standardized sprint and PI readiness checks, and explicit tracking of demo outcomes and follow-up actions.
                  • Strengthen roadmap-to-backlog traceability, INVEST-compliant story quality, and early stakeholder alignment to ensure predictable execution and reduced rework.
                  Delivery Predictability & Flow
                  • Make burndown charts, flow metrics, and release tracking mandatory decision-support tools, reviewed consistently across daily, sprint, and PI ceremonies.
                  • Define and standardize a core set of flow, quality, automation, and test metrics with trusted dashboards to enable faster, data-driven decisions.
                  • Strengthen impediment and risk management by standardizing ROAM usage, enforcing early risk identification, and tracking mitigation actions to closure.
                  • Align estimation techniques, capacity planning, and metric reviews to improve predictability, transparency, and leadership engagement.
                  Product Ownership & Business Alignment
                  • Link explicit, measurable KPIs to PI business goals and cascade them down to sprint goals to reinforce outcome-based execution.
                  • Ensure KPIs are visible, well-understood, and actively used in decision-making rather than treated as passive reporting artifacts.
                  • Track actions from KPI reviews and follow through to ensure tangible business impact and accountability.
                  Team Ways of Working
                  • Engage vendor and partner teams early in pre-PI activities, including solution finalization, epic prioritization, and business value discussions.
                  • Strengthen confidence voting participation and follow-up to improve commitment quality and shared ownership across internal and vendor teams.
